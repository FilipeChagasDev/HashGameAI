How do it works ?

Whenever you make a move, the algorithm does an in-depth search for every possible end of the game from the current state, calculating agent probability of winning and the your probability of winning for every move that the agent can make after your move. The gap that the agent will choose to mark with X will always be the one that gives it the highest probability of winning or not losing.

But this algorithm can fail in the mission to always win. You can win the game.
